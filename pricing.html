Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our free platform

Is Swell really completely free?

Yes! Swell is 100% free with no hidden costs, no premium tiers, and no subscription fees. All features are unlocked from day one and will remain free forever. Usage is limited only by Firebase's generous free tier, which provides ample capacity for most business needs.

How do you make money if it's free?

Swell is a passion project focused on democratizing booking automation. We believe in providing value to the community without monetization barriers.

Are there any usage limits?

Usage is based on Firebase's generous free tier limits, which include substantial quotas for database operations, storage, and bandwidth. These limits are more than sufficient for most businesses - you can handle thousands of bookings monthly without any issues.

What about support and updates?

We provide community support and regular updates to keep Swell running smoothly. Your feedback helps us improve the platform for everyone.

Can I use this for my business?

Absolutely! Swell is designed for businesses of all sizes, from solo entrepreneurs to large enterprises. Commercial use is completely welcome and encouraged.
