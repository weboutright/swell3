Having Issues? Click here for troubleshooting

‚ö†Ô∏è Seeing "This app isn't verified" warning?

This is normal! Google shows this for personal Apps Script projects.

  1. Click "Advanced" (or "Show Advanced")
  2. Click "Go to [Your Project Name] (unsafe)"
  3. Click "Allow" to grant permissions

This is safe because you're deploying YOUR OWN code to YOUR Google account.

‚ùå Deployed with wrong "Execute as" setting?

  4. Change "Execute as" to "User accessing the web app"

What Happens When You Register

  1. Your Gmail + Code.gs URL are saved to the central database
  2. When you visit the admin dashboard later, you'll sign in with your Gmail
  3. The system looks up your Gmail and loads YOUR specific Code.gs
  4. Because of the "User accessing" setting, only YOU can access your data
