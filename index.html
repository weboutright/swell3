Why Swell is Free Forever

I'm tired of subscription services taking over people's lives.

Every month, businesses and individuals are being drained by countless subscription fees for subpar software with half-baked integrations and limited automation. These companies have become payment tyrants, holding your business hostage with monthly and yearly charges that add up to thousands of dollars.

The worst part? Most of these "premium" solutions don't even work properly. You pay $50, $100, or even $500+ per month for booking systems that break, integrations that fail, and customer support that doesn't care because they already have your money.

I believe in people. I believe in your success. I believe that you should keep 100% of what you earn without some corporation skimming off the top every single month for the "privilege" of using basic automation tools.

That's why Swell is completely free, forever. No trials that expire. No "freemium" models that cripple core features. No surprise bills. No corporate greed.

We do accept optional payments from users who want to support the service—these donations help keep Swell running smoothly and allow us to continue improving the platform. But these are entirely voluntary. The core service remains 100% free, always.

Your success shouldn't cost you a subscription. Build your business. Keep your profits. That's how it should be.
